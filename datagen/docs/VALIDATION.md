Validation Guide

This guide captures a set of sanity checks to validate the synthetic omni‑channel dataset generated by the retail datagen. You can run the SQL in docs/validation_queries.sql against the DuckDB at data/retail.duckdb.

- Pricing invariants
  - Cost < SalePrice ≤ MSRP
  - Cost/SalePrice between 0.50 and 0.85 for most products

- Tax checks
  - Store tax_rate in reasonable range (0–10.25%)
  - Reduced‑rate items present in dim_products.taxability
  - Recompute sample receipt taxes: |recorded − computed| ≤ $0.02 for > 98%

- POS behavior
  - Basket size by store_format roughly: express ~2, hypermarket 12–16
  - Returns present (receipt_type='RETURN') and negative quantities in lines

- Inventory fidelity
  - fact_store_inventory_txn includes SALE, INBOUND_SHIPMENT, RETURN, ADJUSTMENT
  - fact_dc_inventory_txn includes INBOUND_SHIPMENT, OUTBOUND_SHIPMENT

- Online orders
  - Fulfillment mix near 60/30/10 (DC/Store/BOPIS)
  - Lines carry PromoCode 10–30% of the time
  - Small cancellation rate present; cancelled orders have zero totals

- Marketing
  - Device multipliers ~ 1.0x/1.2x/1.5x across channels

- Presence/Sensors
  - Foot traffic zones non‑uniform and vary by store_format
  - BLE pings per visit vary by format; ~30% pings link to customers
